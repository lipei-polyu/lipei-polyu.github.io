Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Logistics and Maritime Studies at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Sun Qinghe and Prof. Hans Wang Shuaian.

Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my Master's degree from Southeast University in 2025 under the supervision of Prof. Terry Liu Zhiyuan, and my Bachelor's degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2022.

My research interests primarily focus on Robust Optimization, Deep Learning, and Intelligent Transportation Systems.
